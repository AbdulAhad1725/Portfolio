My Approach

How I design

Every project starts with understanding people — not pixels. Here's my process for turning problems into products.

01
🔍
Discover & Research

Understanding who the users are, what they need, and where current solutions fall short. Personas, user flows, and pain-point mapping.

02
📐
Define & Wireframe

Structuring information architecture and sketching low-fidelity wireframes to map out user journeys before touching visuals.

03
🎨
Design & Prototype

Building interactive prototypes in Figma with consistent design systems — typography, color, spacing — that can be handed off to dev.

04
🧪
Test & Iterate

Validating with real users. Gathering feedback, identifying friction points, and refining until the experience feels effortless.
